About SafeSpace

A next-generation content moderation platform that understands context, intent, and nuance.

🧠

Context-Aware AI

Evaluates the full meaning of statements rather than flagging individual keywords. Casual profanity is distinguished from targeted abuse.

⚡

Async Processing

Queue-based non-blocking architecture lets you submit multiple messages simultaneously without waiting.

🛡️

Prompt Injection Resistant

Robust system-level guards prevent adversarial attempts to override or disable moderation rules.

🎨

Premium UI/UX

Glassmorphism design with dark/light mode, smooth micro-animations, and fully responsive layout.

📊

Full Audit Trail

Every decision is logged with ID, reasoning, timestamp, and execution time for complete transparency.

🔧

Configurable

Customize API keys, model selection, and system prompts through the built-in settings panel.
